This is the recipe for a cake

Description

This recipe includes mixing various ingredients in a specific manner and heating the mixture up in order to create a cake.

This cake can be different flavors (such as vanilla, chocolate, or both) and have different shapes. Below are the ingredients, and steps in order to create your cake.

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Butter
  • Eggs
  • Sugar
  • Salt
  • Milk
  • Baking Soda

Steps

  1. Prepare your ingredients
  2. Grease and preheat
  3. Whisk the dry mix
  4. Cream your butter and sugar
  5. Add the eggs
  6. Combine the ingredients
  7. Pour batter into pan
  8. Bake your cake
  9. Allow cake to cool
  10. Enjoy your cake!
