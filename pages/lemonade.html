This is the recipe for Lemonade

Description

This recipe includes mixing various ingredients in a specific manner in order to make lemonade.

This lemonade can be different flavors (such as pink lemonade, blue lemonade, ect) and have different combinations. Below are the ingredients, and steps in order to create your lemonade.

Ingredients

  • Water
  • Sugar
  • Lemons

Steps

  1. Prepare your ingredients
  2. Slice the lemons in half
  3. Squeeze the lemon juice into a cup
  4. Remove any seeds from the cup
  5. Combine lemon juice with sugar and cold water
  6. Stir
  7. Enjoy your lemonade!
