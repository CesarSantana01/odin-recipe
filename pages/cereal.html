This is the recipe for cereal

Description

This recipe includes mixing various ingredients in a specific manner in order to fix some cereal.

This cereal can be different flavors (such as cornflakes, fruit loops, ect) and have different combinations. Below are the ingredients, and steps in order to create your cereal.

Ingredients

  • Cereal
  • Milk
  • Sugar(optional)

Steps

  1. Prepare your ingredients
  2. Get a bowl
  3. Pour some cereal
  4. Pour some milk
  5. Add some sugar(optional)
  6. Stir with spoon
  7. Enjoy your cereal!
